About This Framework

This evaluation is built on the 6-Stage Growth Loop — a GTM framework designed around one core principle: every stage, executed well, makes the next stage cheaper and more effective. The result is a compounding system where early investments in awareness reduce the cost of conversion, and retention fuels the top of the funnel.

The Compounding Logic
Cold outreach converts better when prospects already recognize the brand. Each stage makes the next one cheaper.
The Signal Overlap
Your paid audiences and your outbound lists overlap more than most people realize. Every engagement sharpens the next campaign.
The Retention Loop
Clients who stay become case studies. Case studies become your best top-of-funnel. Stage 6 feeds Stage 1.

Tool Recommendations — Closing the Biggest Gaps
STAGE 01 · C+
Generate Demand — Brand Awareness Gap
LinkedIn CTV
Serves video ads on streaming platforms to LinkedIn-matched audiences. Reaches CIOs and home office buyers while they're watching TV — not just while they're on LinkedIn. Cost-effective and already in LinkedIn's ad suite.
Metadata.io
Automates paid campaign experimentation across LinkedIn, Facebook, and Google simultaneously. Finds the highest-converting audience and creative combinations faster than manual testing — ideal for a lean team running multiple channels.
Letterdrop
Repurposes long-form content into LinkedIn posts, newsletters, and SEO articles automatically. Extends content reach without adding headcount — directly addresses the brand re-education gap Lauck flagged.
STAGE 03 · C+
Stay in the Room — Orchestration Gap
Common Room
Aggregates engagement signals from LinkedIn, Reddit, email, and events into one view. Shows which enterprise accounts are warming up across every channel simultaneously — closes the orchestration gap without replacing existing tools.
Factors.ai
Connects anonymous website behavior to known accounts, surfaces which firms are engaging across touchpoints, and feeds that data into HubSpot. Fills the gap between Influ2's contact-level data and website engagement that currently goes untracked.
Kit (fka ConvertKit)
Purpose-built for creator-style newsletters targeting professional audiences. An enterprise-specific newsletter — think Jerome's Asset Management Pulse, rebuilt with reach — keeps YCharts in the room between touchpoints without relying on paid media.
STAGE 04 · B-
Earn the Trust — Proof Asset Deployment Gap
Loom / Vidyard
Short-form async video for sales enablement and personalized outreach. A 90-second Loom from Lauck's team referencing a prospect's specific firm situation — paired with a relevant case study — closes the trust gap faster than any email sequence.
Testimonial.to
Collects and displays video testimonials from customers at scale. Directly addresses the C-suite testimonial gap — a CSO or CIO saying on video what the enterprise page needs to say is more powerful than any copywriter.
Mutiny
Personalizes the enterprise landing page dynamically based on the visitor's firm, industry, or account — without engineering support. A broker-dealer sees compliance messaging. An RIA sees proposal generation messaging. One page, multiple value props.
STAGE 05 · B
Score and Route — Attribution Gap
Clay
Enriches inbound leads automatically with firmographic data, intent signals, and ICP scoring — then routes them to HubSpot or Salesforce with context already attached. Removes the manual scoring step and ensures enterprise leads get to Lauck's team faster and better qualified.
Attributer
Captures UTM parameters and channel data on every form fill and passes it into HubSpot automatically. Solves the attribution rebuild problem at Stage 5 without engineering resources — immediately clarifies which campaigns are driving enterprise pipeline.
Dreamdata
B2B revenue attribution platform that maps every marketing touchpoint across the full enterprise sales cycle — including the 12-month window YCharts uses for enterprise leads. Shows exactly which channels and campaigns influenced closed revenue, not just last-touch conversions.

The One Thing That Ties It All Together

Beau said it directly — they've done a great job standing up individual channels but the missing piece is gluing them into one cohesive enterprise story. That's the compounding logic from the playbook. Right now each stage is working but not feeding the next one efficiently. Stage 3 is the critical gap — without a cohesive nurture narrative connecting demand to conversion, the loop never compounds. That's the job. That's what Jerome built the foundation for and what the enterprise marketing hire would build on top of.
